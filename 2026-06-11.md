- 🏢 **[Anthropic Drops Claude Fable 5 — Mythos-Class Power, Now Available to Everyone](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-fable-5-mythos-5)** — Anthropic launched Claude Fable 5, the first publicly available Mythos-class model, across its API, Amazon Bedrock, Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry at $10/$50 per million input/output tokens with a 1M token context window. For enterprise mobility and IT teams, this is the moment AI assistants shift from standalone apps to the operating system layer — and Apple's privacy-first architecture could become the default template for regulated industries.

For any global enterprise with multilingual teams or customer support operations, this materially changes what real-time translation infrastructure looks like — and Grab is already testing it across 10M+ monthly voice calls.
